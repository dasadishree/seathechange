Our Mission

We hope to clean and purify the shores of Long Island, and later other locations, as much as possible. Our mission is to help our environment be better, as we conserve our oceans and seas, including all wildlife within it. Our organization promotes no-kill shelters and works on animal welfare. Check out our blog on euthanasia and animal shelters for more info!
